## How to Use Practice Tests Effectively

### Don't just practice — practice smart:

1. **Simulate real conditions.** Use the timer. Don't pause. Don't look things up. The more realistic your practice, the more prepared you'll be.

2. **Review every piece of feedback.** After each exercise, read the AI feedback carefully. Don't just check your score — understand WHY you scored that way.

3. **Track your patterns.** Do you always lose points on vocabulary? Grammar? Coherence? Identifying your consistent weaknesses focuses your study time.

4. **Space your practice.** Doing 1 exercise per day for 30 days beats doing 30 exercises in one weekend. Your brain needs time to consolidate.

5. **Focus on your weakest section.** If Writing is your CLB 7 and Listening is your CLB 9, spend 70% of your time on Writing.
